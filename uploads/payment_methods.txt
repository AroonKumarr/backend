E-Challan can be paid through JazzCash, EasyPaisa, or 1Link ATM services.
Manual challan payments can be made at ITP Headquarters F-8 Markaz or any designated traffic zone office.
Online payments are available through the Islamabad City App and Pakistan Citizen Portal.
ITP also accepts challan payments through HBL Konnect and Allied Bank digital banking platforms.
Cash payments are accepted at National Bank branches designated for traffic fines.
Fine receipts are generated automatically and sent via SMS after payment confirmation.
Citizens can verify challan status online using CNIC or vehicle registration number.
E-receipts can be downloaded directly from the ITP portal after payment.
Late payments may incur a penalty fee of Rs. 200 after 30 days.
Payments made through JazzCash or EasyPaisa update in the system within 10 minutes.
In case of payment failure, users are advised to contact ITP Helpline at 1915.
Bulk fine payments for companies or organizations are available through ITP corporate services.
E-Challan QR codes can be scanned for instant payment verification.
Citizens are encouraged to use online methods to avoid long queues at traffic offices.
All challan payments are recorded in the Islamabad Police central database.
Duplicate receipts can be requested by visiting any ITP office.
For refunds due to duplicate payments, an application can be submitted to ITP Finance Section.
Receipts and challan records can be exported in PDF from the ITP web portal.
ITP encourages citizens to verify their challan payment history regularly.
Failure to pay challan within 60 days may result in vehicle impoundment.
